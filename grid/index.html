How it's built

Method & caveats

Carbon intensity, two ways

The big "right now" number weights the live fuel mix by emission factors (coal 0.98, gas 0.49, distillate 0.85 tCO₂/MWh; renewables and storage zero). It's an estimate, because settled emissions lag real time.

The 120-day trend uses the operator's own settled emissions over energy — the authoritative figure. On the latest overlapping day the two agree to within about 2%, which is the check that keeps the live gauge honest.

What this is and isn't

Battery discharge counts as generation; charging and pumped-hydro load are excluded so the mix doesn't double-count. Bioenergy is treated carbon-neutral, per the national greenhouse convention — debatable, but standard.

This is the wholesale grid, not your retail plan: rooftop solar that never hits the market isn't fully here, and emission factors are fleet averages, not per-station. Refreshes through the day; numbers settle as the operator finalises them.
