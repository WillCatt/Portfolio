The price of a starting XI

Transfer markets are a crowd-sourced strength rating: thousands of analysts and fans haggling over what a player is worth. Add up a nation's best eleven and you get a quick, brutal read on the gap between the favourites and the field. Here's every 2026 squad priced by Transfermarkt — and every group game as a value mismatch you can open up. Browse the fixtures, click any match, see the two XIs side by side.
