The road to the final

The group stage is done; thirty-two teams remain. The same Dixon-Coles model — frozen the day before the tournament opened, the very ratings the Forecast piece is graded on — reads the whole knockout draw. Every tie gets a chance of going through; the bracket gets rolled forward forty-thousand times to ask the only question that matters now: who lifts the trophy? Click any tie to open it up.

Part I

The ladder

The knockout bracket

Each tie shows the model's chance that each side advances — a knockout has no draws, so a level game after 90 minutes is taken to extra time and, if needed, penalties (close to a coin flip). The brighter, bolder name is the favourite. Played ties lock to their real result and the projection re-sharpens. Tap a tie for the full read.
